# Data for Chapter 7: Model Selection

This page lists the datasets used in the R scripts for this chapter.

## Datasets

### btw2021kreis.csv
- **Description**: Data from the 2021 German federal election (Bundestagswahl) at the district level.
- **File**: [[Download]](../data/btw2021kreis.csv)
- **Usage**: Used in `factor.R` for factor analysis of election results.

## Scripts in this Chapter
- **aic-large-n.R**: Discusses AIC behavior with large sample sizes.
- **aic-small-n.R**: Discusses AIC behavior with small sample sizes.
- **penalisation.R**: Illustrates penalization optimization.
- **factor.R**: Performs factor analysis on the 2021 German election data.
